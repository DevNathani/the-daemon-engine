The window is jammed shut. Beyond the glass, the full moon washes the forest in pale light.

This window can still be opened… unlike that other Windows.
At least this one just gets stuck, you push it, it resists, but eventually it might give in.

The blue Windows, though? One small task and suddenly it freezes, updates itself, restarts without consent, and leaves you staring at a screen like your life choices led you here.

Honestly, this wooden window is more reliable.
At least it doesn’t show me a blue screen and say, “Your night has run into a problem.”
